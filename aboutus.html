Who we are: Citizen Scientists

We are scientists and researchers who believe in extreme citizen science ie, the practice of involving citizens in solving the intractable, social problems that plague their respective societies eg, preventable deaths and injuries from boda-boda related accidents (motorbikes).

What we do: emergency care

1SMILE works to Repurpose boda boda commuters' excess capacities and Retask last mile care infrastructure to Retool first responders.

What we offer: value proposition

The 1SMILE project enables citizen scientists to catalyse 1mn smiles by curating 1mn care sites around accident blackspots and contributing 1mn stories about first responders.

What we stand for: Core Values

What we stand for

1SMILE stands for 6 CORES Approach to care innovation ie,
C - Creativity (the ability to create and innovate). 1SMILE is a digital space that provides the tools and resources to spark spocial innovations along the emergency care value chain. We encourage creative destruction and creative imitation;
O - Opportunity (by having the space, resources and support). SMILE supports the creative opportunity by providing tools, the location, a collaborative space, assistance, and many layers of support for STEAM (Science, technology, Engineering, Analytics and Mathematics) related projects;
R - Relationship (involvement with other peers and faculty). SMILE is a collaborative learning space where new relationships are formed and existing relationships supported around STEAM promotion;
E - Entrepreneurial (the ability to create something new and to move it from concept to reality). The creative spark in SMILE is the engine for the entrepreneurial engagement and
S - Skill Building (the ability to try something new and/or develop skills with tools, technologies, and processes). As a collaborative space for the engagement and creation of STEAM-related projects, SMILE will support skill building by providing a digital platform, tools, a lab environment, educational/training support, and information on techniques for SMILE consumers in a robust and engaged work environment.
